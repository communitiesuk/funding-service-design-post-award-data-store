1.3.2. How much capital funding are you requesting from COF25?

Capital costs are the costs of buying or leasing your asset and paying for refurbishment. You can apply for up to £2 million (

£2000000

) in capital costs from COF25.

Enter the amount using only numerals, for example, 1500000

1.3.3. Do you plan to request any revenue funding?

Revenue costs go towards running the asset once you've taken ownership of it. This might include general costs, including for:

  • utilities
  • staffing

You can apply for up to 20% of your capital funding request or £50,000, whichever is smaller.

1.3.4. Do you plan to secure match funding? (either cash or in-kind)

Match funding means funding covered by you or another funder. This can be cash, or in-kind (non-cash, for example, donations of goods or services).
